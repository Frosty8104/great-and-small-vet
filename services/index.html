Puppy Wellness Plan

Hello, and welcome to your pet’s wellness plan. Everything listed below is 20% off, and we include spay and neuter services if required. We aim to make sure your pet is as healthy as he or she can be at affordable prices!

Spay/Neuter

Spaying or neutering your pet helps avoid breeding-related problems, as well as genetic issues that can be passed down from mother to puppies or kittens. It will also prevent your pet from contributing to the current overpopulation problem. Spay and neuter services include:

  • Preanesthetic physical exam/blood test
  • Spay or neuter surgery
  • Post-op and post-anesthetic care

Vaccine Schedule

Vaccines offer extra protection against disease infection. As the antibodies from the mother’s colostrum wanes, vaccines are given to help your puppy create its own immunity by stimulating its immune system with modified live, recombinant, or killed pathogens.
