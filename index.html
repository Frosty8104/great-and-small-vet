Pet of the Month

Dharra is an Arabian mare of Al Khamsa lineage. The Al Khamsa story is told that Muhammed released his mares to drink after a long journey and called them back before they reached the water. Only five mares came back before reaching water, therefore these five mares are called Al Khamsa, or The Five, and every Al Khamsa Arabian descends from these five mares.
